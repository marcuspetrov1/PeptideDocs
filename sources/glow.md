GLOW

Alright — the “Glow” blend people talk about in peptide circles is usually a skin-/repair-focused combo, most commonly:

Most Glow 70 mg formulas are still a combo of:
	•	GHK-Cu (skin, collagen, hair)
	•	BPC-157 (repair, anti-inflammatory)
	•	TB-500 (systemic healing)

🧪 Your reconstitution math

70 mg total peptides + 3 mL bacteriostatic water

70 mg ÷ 3 mL = 23.3 mg per mL
