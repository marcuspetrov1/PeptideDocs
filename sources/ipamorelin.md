💉 Common research-style ranges (non-prescriptive)

Ipamorelin is a short-acting GH secretagogue often studied for pulsatile growth hormone release.

Typical ranges people reference:

👉 0.1–0.3 mg per administration

Some research setups explore multiple smaller doses instead of one larger dose.

⏰ When it’s typically studied

Ipamorelin is often looked at when GH naturally peaks:

✔ Before bed
OR
✔ Fasted (morning or between meals)

Avoid:

❌ Right after high-carb meals (insulin may blunt GH response)
